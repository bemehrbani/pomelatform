Executive Summary

This agreement outlines a co-founder partnership between Pomegroup.studio (represented by Mahdi Farimani) and Mustafa Sabar to develop, launch, and scale SabarKoti.

The Vision: Transitioning Sabar Palvelut's service operations into a tech-enabled brand called SabarKoti. We will establish complete digital independence by launching a custom booking system, restoring domain administration, and integrating native Finnish household tax deduction (kotitalousvähennys) capabilities.

Pomegroup.studio will act as the technical co-founder and strategic digital partner, providing full end-to-end design, database infrastructure, Stripe payment gateways, and client acquisition funnels.

Pomegroup.studio Commitments & Scope

Upon execution of this contract, Pomegroup.studio will build and manage the following core infrastructure for SabarKoti:

1. Identity & Rebranding

Full rebranding from Sabar Palvelut to SabarKoti, including modern logo marks, typographic scale, and digital visual assets.

2. Domain & Email Setup

Registering and configuring the new domain sabarkoti.fi. Launching independent business emails (Google Workspace) secure from previous contractors.

3. Automated Booking Portal

Deploying the custom online booking funnel (built on Next.js/Supabase/Stripe) featuring the auto-generated tax-deduction receipt engine.

4. Operations Dashboard

Building a live management portal for SabarKoti's administrators to schedule assignments and manage staff bookings.

Ownership & Intellectual Property

Pomegroup.studio retains intellectual property rights for the custom booking software and platform codebase. SabarKoti receives a perpetual, exclusive license to run its business operations on this code for the duration of the partnership.

2

Equity & Profit Share

Equity stakes denote rights to annual profit distributions (dividends) and ownership in the event of an acquisition or business restructuring. This will be officially drafted into a Finnish shareholder agreement (osakassopimus) within 30 days of signing.

3

Contract Termination

Either party may terminate this monthly service agreement with a 60-day written notice. Any accrued equity is subject to standard vesting rules to be detailed in the shareholder agreement.
